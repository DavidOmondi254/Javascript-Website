About Me

A story of good

Sirkelly Foundation started 14years ago upon realization that there were many agencies offering scholarships to bright underprivileged kids. We carried out research through Hallmark Consulting Group and the findings indicate many deserving children were not scoring the mark that would put them on the short list for consideration due to debilitating social challenges. We then decided at SKF to climb down the supply chain to reach out to those kids who are naturally gifted but are suffocated by indescribable difficulties in their social environment. We extend the hand of support in a small way, provide them with basic needs and give assurance that 'mbele iko sawa' it has since worked magic. Over 200 children are now in various secondary schools enjoying prestigious scholarships. We also mentor in high school, monitor their performance to ensure it's aligned to their career aspirations. So far we have a sizable number pursuing medicine, nursing and engineering in various universities. We further guide on their prospective professional careers, life skills and socialise them to dream of pursuing their education abroad. We have now five Brave Winners who have secured scholarships abroad and will be flying out before the end of the year 2023. Sirkelly Foundation is a garden of golden dreams as our motto aptly states: WE HAVE A DREAM.
